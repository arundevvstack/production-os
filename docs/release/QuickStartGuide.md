# DP Creative OS — Quick Start Guide

## Creating Your First Project

1. **Dashboard**: Navigate to `/production/projects` and click "New Project".
2. **Script Studio**: Enter a script title and text. Click **"Lock Script"** in the sidebar. *You cannot proceed until the script is locked.*
3. **Storyboard**: Navigate to the Storyboard. Click "Add Scene". 
4. **Shot List**: Inside the Scene, click "Add Shot". Specify camera angles and lighting requirements.
5. **Generation Studio**: Select the Shot and open the Studio. Enter a prompt, select your preferred Model, and click "Generate".
6. **Asset Approval**: Once the asset returns, click "Approve" in the Asset review bar. This will ripple completion metrics up to the Project dashboard.
