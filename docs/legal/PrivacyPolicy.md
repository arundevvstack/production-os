# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 1, 2026

## 1. Data Collection
DP Creative OS collects production metadata, AI generation prompts, and user account details strictly for the purpose of executing the Creative Graph Engine workflows.

## 2. Third-Party AI Providers
By using the Generation Studio, you agree that your prompts and selected Image Assets will be transmitted to third-party AI providers (e.g., OpenRouter, OpenAI, Anthropic). We do not use your generated assets to train our own proprietary models.

## 3. Data Retention
Workspace data is retained indefinitely as part of the Creative Memory Engine unless explicitly deleted by a Workspace Administrator. Deleting a workspace invokes a cascade delete across all associated relational tables.
